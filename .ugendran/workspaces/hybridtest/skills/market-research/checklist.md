# Market Research — Quality Gate Checklist

> Definition of done for the `market-research` skill. Every applicable item must be
> checked, or waived with a one-line recorded reason. Unchecked, unwaived items
> block delivery.

**Skill:** `market-research` · **Category:** Product · **Version:** 1.0.0

## Discover

- [ ] Problem statement and success criteria are written down
- [ ] Stakeholders and their concerns are identified
- [ ] Constraints (time, budget, tech, compliance) are explicit
- [ ] Existing artifacts reviewed for reuse

## Specify

- [ ] Intended outcome captured as a reviewable spec
- [ ] Scope and non-goals stated
- [ ] Assumptions and open questions listed

## Design — domain coverage

- [ ] Addressed: TAM / SAM / SOM market sizing (top-down and bottom-up)
- [ ] Addressed: competitive landscape and feature/positioning matrices
- [ ] Addressed: customer segmentation, jobs-to-be-done, and demand signals
- [ ] Addressed: pricing and willingness-to-pay analysis
- [ ] Addressed: trend, regulatory, and PESTEL scanning

## Validate — anti-patterns avoided

- [ ] Confirmed avoided: cherry-picking data that confirms the desired conclusion
- [ ] Confirmed avoided: citing TAM without a credible bottom-up cross-check
- [ ] Confirmed avoided: treating a few customer interviews as statistically representative
- [ ] Spec is testable — acceptance criteria are objective
- [ ] Reviewed by domain expert(s)
- [ ] Risks identified with mitigations

## Deliver — artifacts

- [ ] Produced: Market sizing model with assumptions
- [ ] Produced: Competitive matrix and positioning map
- [ ] Produced: Segment profiles and JTBD
- [ ] Produced: Go / no-go recommendation with evidence
- [ ] Final artifact stored in the spec registry
- [ ] Owner and review cadence assigned
- [ ] Handoff / communication done

## Sign-off

- [ ] All gates above checked or waived (with reason recorded)
- [ ] Artifact peer-reviewed by at least one other person
- [ ] Decisions/trade-offs captured (ADR or decision log)
- [ ] Owner and review cadence assigned
- [ ] Linked from the project's spec index / `openspec/` registry
